Skill Forge: Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

Project Goal: To provide students with an immediate, data-driven understanding of their personalized learning and behavior profile, enabling them to make informed decisions about their study approach and skill development.

Core Feature: Learning Genome Assessment

A 10-question, interactive quiz designed to capture a holistic view of the user's learning characteristics.

Assessment Categories (7-Point Analysis):
  • Thinking Style: Analytical, Intuitive, or Structured approach to problem-solving.
  • Perception: Primary learning modality (Visual, Auditory, Kinesthetic).
  • Pace of Assimilation: Speed and method of grasping new, challenging material.
  • Core Interests: Primary inclination toward Technology/Logic, Arts/Expression, or Social/Business.
  • Emotional State: Stability and adaptability when faced with stress or unexpected changes.
  • Strong & Weak Skills: Identification of natural aptitudes and areas requiring targeted practice.
  • Task Behavior: Tendency toward planning, procrastination, resilience, and group role (leadership/support).

MVP Deliverable: The Digital Profile

  • Profile Scorecard: A grid summarizing the results across the 7 criteria.
  • Identified Growth Areas: A bulleted list highlighting key weaknesses and behavioral patterns that could hinder learning.
  • Personalized Recommendation: A clear, concise statement suggesting immediate areas of focus or the most suitable learning path based on the profile.

Out of Scope (Future Features)

  • User Accounts & Data Persistence: Saving and tracking profiles over time.
  • Module Integration: Assigning specific course modules or external resources based on the recommendation.
  • Multi-User Comparison: Functionality to compare one's profile with peers or ideal career profiles.
